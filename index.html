About Rajasthan Silica Quartz Suppliers

Your trusted partner for premium silica quartz solutions

Leading Silica Quartz Supplier Since 2010

Based in the mineral-rich state of Rajasthan, India, we are a premier supplier of high-purity silica quartz with operations spanning across major mining areas including Udaipur, Bharatpur, Bhilwara, Rajsamand, and Dausa.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in providing superior quality silica quartz products that meet the highest international standards, supporting industries in their pursuit of excellence and innovation.

Mining Heritage

Rajasthan's geological formations provide some of the world's purest silica quartz deposits. Our strategic location and deep understanding of local geology enable us to extract premium quality raw materials.

Our Plant & Process

Advanced processing techniques for superior quality

Extraction

Selective mining from premium quartz deposits in Rajasthan using modern equipment

Crushing & Screening

Multi-stage crushing and precision screening to achieve desired particle sizes

Washing & Scrubbing

Intensive washing and attrition scrubbing to remove impurities and surface contamination

Magnetic Separation

High-intensity magnetic separation to remove iron oxides and magnetic impurities

Flotation Processing

Chemical flotation to separate mica, feldspar and other non-magnetic impurities

Drying & Classification

Controlled drying and final classification to meet exact customer specifications

Quality Testing

Comprehensive lab testing including purity analysis, particle size distribution, and chemical composition

Packaging & Export

Professional packaging in various sizes from 25kg bags to bulk containers for export
